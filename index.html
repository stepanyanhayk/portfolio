HAYK STEPANYAN

I am a third-year undergraduate student at the Georgia Institute of Technology studying Computer Science with a concentration on smart devices and artificial intelligence. I am passionate about using technologies to find solutions for real-life problems. In particular, my technical interests include large-scale software systems, smart devices, autonomous machines, and financial tech.

I was born and raised in Armenia, where I went to PhysMath specialized school. In my free time, I enjoy playing football and chess, hiking, and traveling. Here is my digital travel diary.

Last summer, I was a software engineer intern at Meta, where I developed user-interest based and personalized generators for the Facebook Group Reels and Video distribution/recommendation system.

In 2021, I was a research engineer at HiLearn, helping to develop an AI-automated financial advising application.

I have also been fortunate to work as an undergraduate researcher on GTSfM, under the supervision of Dr. Frank Dellaert in the School of Interactive Computing.
