HAYK STEPANYAN

I am a second-year student from Armenia studying Computer Science at the Georgia Institute of Technology. I am passionate about artificial intelligence and its applications in finding solutions for real-life problems. In particular, I am interested in large-scale systems, smart devices, autonomous vehicles, and financial tech.

I am also an undergraduate researcher at the School of Interactive Computing working on AutoRally, an autonomous RC car, under the supervision of Dr. James Rehg and Ph.D. student Jason Gibson.

Before that, I was a research engineer at HiLearn, where I worked on the long-term market (stocks, bonds, funds) simulation.
