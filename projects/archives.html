DATABASE APPLICATION FOR THE ARCHIVES

In 2018, after finding out that one of the departments of the National Archives of Armenia uses papers and text files to store most of its important data, I volunteered to build a database application to make the data circulation more secure and efficient.

The final product was approved by the senior management and was represented to employees together with the user guide and technical documentation.

I also submitted the application as my final project for the Pearson International A-Level Applied Information and Communication Technology class and received the highest A* mark.
