FOOTBALL

In 2019, Armenia hosted the final tournament of the UEFA European U-19 Football Championship. During the two weeks of the tournament, I was the team liaison officer and translator of the Italy U-19 team, where I got the unique chance to work with world-famous football coaches and players, such as Mirco Gasparetto, Manuel Locatelli, Marco Carnesecchi and Paulo Gozzi.

My responsibilities included communicating with the team manager and coaches, arranging the transportation, advising how to plan daily schedules, booking training fields, solving organizational problems connected with the hotels and stadiums. I not only improved my planning, communication, management, fast decision-making, and non-standard problem-solving skills but also gave me new friends and connections worldwide.
